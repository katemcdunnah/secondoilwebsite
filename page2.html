Planning Process

All things begin as thoughts and ideas. Therefore, before you begin painting, you must have an idea. Once you form an idea, it's beneficial to you to create a few sketches of your idea. Once you've created your sketches, you can then get a feel of what you want to paint. You can choose your favorite sketch, or even combine some of your sketches to. Also before painting, you need to make sure you have everything you need to paint. Some items you will need will be listed below.

Materials Needed

  • Canvas
  • Oil paints
  • Paint thinner or turpentine
  • Gel
  • Paint brushes
  • Jar
  • Piece of cloth/rag
  • Palette
